01 / Overview

Analyzed raw operational data to redesign the company's centralized supply chain into a customer-facing product experience, then presented data-backed strategic recommendations to a panel of Deloitte judges.

Competed in a Deloitte-sponsored case competition, working with a team as product strategy consultants for GreenStar Technologies, a global chipset module manufacturer.

02 / Key Highlights

01 Data Analysis

Cleaned and analyzed messy operational datasets across shipments, inventory, demand, visibility, and financials to quantify the scale of GreenStar's supply chain pain points

02 Strategy

Developed a product-centric supply chain strategy with a phased rollout that prioritized quick-win MVPs while building toward long-term scalability across customer tiers

03 Delivery

Defined success metrics (KPIs) tied to customer reliability, cost savings, and adoption, and delivered the recommendation in a polished 10-minute final presentation

A supply chain isn't infrastructure. It's a product experience.
